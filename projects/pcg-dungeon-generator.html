University Dissertation Artifact

PCG Dungeon Generator

Platform: PC

Engine: Unity

Development Time: 6 Months

Role: Solo Developer

Tools: C#, Unity

This project was developed as part of my university dissertation and explores procedural dungeon generation techniques to create replayable, readable dungeon layouts.

Gameplay Mechanics

The generator utilises multiple PCG approaches including room-based generation, cellular automata, and corridor linking to ensure logical dungeon flow.

Key Responsibilities & Contributions

  • Designed and implemented PCG algorithms
  • Developed visual debugging tools
  • Conducted academic research and testing
  • Optimised dungeon readability and flow

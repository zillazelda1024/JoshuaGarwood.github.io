University Dissertation Artifact

PCG Dungeon Generator

Project Details

  • Platform: PC
  • Engine: Unity
  • Development Time: 6 Months
  • Role: Solo Developer
  • Tools: C#, Unity

Project Overview

For my university dissertation, I developed a PC-based procedural content generation (PCG) artifact focused on dungeon generation techniques commonly used in game development.

The project explores how different algorithms affect layout, readability, and player navigation.

Gameplay Mechanics

The project implements Cellular Automata, Binary Space Partitioning, Perlin Noise, Recursive Division, Drunkard Walk, and Recursive Backtracking.

Key Responsibilities & Contributions

  • Designed and implemented procedural generation algorithms
  • Conducted academic research

Project Overview

In Haunted House Ride, players experience a thrilling arcade-style shooting game where they navigate through a spooky theme park attraction. The core gameplay revolves around shooting targets that emerge unpredictably from the haunted environment, requiring quick reflexes and precision to achieve the highest score possible. Players are challenged by a variety of targets, each designed to pop up in dynamic patterns, enhancing the gameplay's excitement and unpredictability. The scoring system rewards players for accuracy and speed, encouraging them to refine their skills and compete against others for top scores. The game’s immersive atmosphere is complemented by responsive gameplay mechanics, making every shot count and adding a layer of intensity to the overall experience. Players can also track their progress through leaderboards, fostering a competitive spirit and motivating them to improve their performance over time.

Gameplay & Mechanics:

Dead Meme offers players an engaging and humorous arcade experience where they navigate a haunted house theme park ride, shooting at whimsical targets that pop up unexpectedly. The game features a dynamic scoring system where each target is assigned different point values based on its difficulty to hit, encouraging players to strategize their shots for maximum points. As players progress, they encounter a variety of comical enemies and obstacles, each designed to enhance the lighthearted theme while maintaining a fast-paced shooting experience. The targets are programmed to appear in a randomized fashion, creating an unpredictable and challenging environment that promotes competitive play among friends. Additionally, players can earn power-ups throughout the ride, adding depth to the gameplay and enhancing overall engagement. Through these mechanics, Dead Meme combines fun, strategy, and humor, providing players with a delightful gaming experience.

Key Responsibilities & Contributions:

3D Model Creation:

Designed and created all 3D models for the game using Blender, including the haunted house environment, props, and target characters. Focused on creating visually compelling assets that enhance the eerie atmosphere of the ride.

Collaborative Development:

Worked closely with a team of four other university students, contributing to the overall design and development of the game. Collaborated on gameplay mechanics, level design, and user experience to ensure a cohesive and enjoyable game.

Asset Integration:

Integrated 3D models into Unreal Engine 5, ensuring that all assets were optimized for performance while maintaining high visual quality. Worked on textures and materials to enhance the realism and immersion of the game environment.

Gameplay Mechanics:

Contributed to the design of gameplay mechanics, including target behavior and scoring systems. Ensured that the targets appeared in a dynamic and challenging manner, encouraging players to improve their skills and compete for high scores.

Project Management:

Participated in regular team meetings to discuss progress, brainstorm ideas, and address challenges. Helped organize tasks and ensure that the project remained on schedule over the three-month development period.

Monster Designs:

I developed a series of monster target designs that are intended to emerge from various hiding spots, such as behind cover or around corners, with the goal of creating jump-scare moments for players. Each target is assigned a distinct point value based on its difficulty and visibility, encouraging players to prioritize their shots strategically. This design approach not only enhances the gameplay experience but also adds an element of excitement as players aim to maximize their scores by hitting higher-value targets.
